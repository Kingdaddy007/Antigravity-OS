# Motion Library Semantic Index

Use this index after the communication job, stillness alternative, and constraints are known. It routes to candidate families; it never makes an effect compulsory.

## Decision Record

Before browsing, write:

- **Job:** What must the visitor understand, notice, compare, or feel?
- **Evidence:** Why motion belongs to the selected concept.
- **Stillness alternative:** The simplest non-motion solution.
- **Constraints:** Content, assets, stack, mobile, reduced motion, performance, and maintenance.
- **Failure condition:** What would make the motion harmful or unnecessary.

## Selection Rule

Compare no more than three candidates. Recommend one of:

1. **Adapt a library candidate** — semantic fit is strong and costs are acceptable.
2. **Build a simpler custom behavior** — the job is real but the library candidates are excessive.
3. **Use stillness** — composition, sequence, or interaction communicates better without motion.

Record what is borrowed, changed, rejected, and how the fallback preserves meaning.
