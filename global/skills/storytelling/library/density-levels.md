# Density Levels

How much is happening on screen at once. Density affects pacing, attention,
and emotional impact.

---

## MINIMAL

**Feeling:** Sparse, generous whitespace, one idea per section.
**When to use:** Luxury, wellness, intimate brands, confident brands.
**Characteristics:**
- Large typography (48-72px+ headlines)
- Generous padding (96-128px section padding)
- One focal point per section
- Lots of breathing room
- Few elements, high impact

**Examples:**
- Apple product pages
- Luxury fashion editorial
- Wellness/coaching landing pages

**Technical approach:**
- Grid: Single column or 2-column max
- Spacing: 96-128px section padding, 48-64px element gaps
- Typography: Large headlines, generous line-height (1.3-1.5)
- Animation: One strong reveal per section, not multiple

---

## MODERATE

**Feeling:** Balanced, structured, clear hierarchy.
**When to use:** Most brands, SaaS, professional services, restaurants.
**Characteristics:**
- Medium typography (32-48px headlines)
- Standard padding (48-96px section padding)
- Clear hierarchy (headline → body → CTA)
- Structured sections with consistent rhythm
- Multiple elements per section, well-organized

**Examples:**
- SaaS landing pages
- Professional services sites
- Restaurant websites

**Technical approach:**
- Grid: 2-3 columns, structured layout
- Spacing: 48-96px section padding, 24-48px element gaps
- Typography: Medium headlines, standard line-height (1.2-1.4)
- Animation: Staggered reveals within sections

---

## MAXIMALIST

**Feeling:** Dense, layered, rich detail, multiple elements.
**When to use:** Creative agencies, gaming, immersive experiences, experimental brands.
**Characteristics:**
- Smaller typography (24-36px headlines) with more text
- Tighter padding (24-48px section padding)
- Multiple focal points per section
- Rich detail, layered elements
- Dense information, high engagement

**Examples:**
- Creative agency portfolios
- Gaming launch sites
- Immersive storytelling experiences

**Technical approach:**
- Grid: 3-4 columns, complex layouts
- Spacing: 24-48px section padding, 16-24px element gaps
- Typography: Smaller headlines, tighter line-height (1.1-1.3)
- Animation: Multiple reveals per section, complex timelines
