# The Voided Protagonist

**Source:** The Silent Patient • Alex Michaelides • 2019 • Psychological Thriller
**Motion personality:** Brutal
**Density:** Minimal
**Psychological lever:** Curiosity gap + information asymmetry

---

## How It Works

Withhold the protagonist. Build an entire world of narrators without a central
anchor. Every voice is one degree removed from the void at the center. The
absence IS the hook.

**In the book:** Alicia Berenson, a famous painter, shoots her husband and then
never speaks again. Multiple narrators orbit around her silence. The void at
the center IS the hook.

**In web design:** Design around an empty center. Let the absence drive curiosity.

---

## Web Translation

**Hero / above-the-fold:**
- Full-bleed layout — large white space with elegant serif text
- Headline: "She hasn't spoken in years."
- Image of subject with face deliberately cropped out

**Spatial / 3D / canvas:**
- R3F scene — sculpted bust centered and lit
- Camera orbits automatically
- On scroll, sculpted head physically splits into fragments via disintegration shader
- Void at center revealed as negative space

**Scroll pacing:**
- Slow reveal, 4x viewport heights to crisis point
- expo.out easing
- Micro-animation: each page section loads with visible 0.5s delay on text fade-in
- Suggests a pause before the subject will "speak"

**Micro-copy & CTA:**
- CTA appears only after void at center fully revealed
- Example: "Unmute Her" — direct, bold, center-screen
