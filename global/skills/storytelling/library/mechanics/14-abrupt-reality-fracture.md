# Abrupt Reality Fracture

**Source:** Dark Matter • Blake Crouch • 2016 • Sci-Fi Thriller
**Motion personality:** Surgical
**Density:** Moderate
**Psychological lever:** Pattern violation + information asymmetry

---

## How It Works

Establish a cozy, safe baseline, then violently interrupt it. Force the user
into a relentless chase to understand their new reality. The shift itself is the hook.

**In the book:** Chapter 1 establishes domestic baseline — cooking dinner for family.
End of Ch.1: abducted at gunpoint. Ch.2-3: disorientation as protagonist wakes in
a world where he's a celebrated hero, not a professor.

**In web design:** Immediate, high-contrast shift from "safe" baseline to dynamic,
high-stakes state. The transition IS the experience.

---

## Web Translation

**Hero / above-the-fold:**
- Split-screen layout
- Headline: "Are you happy with your life?"
- The "other" life is withheld

**Spatial / 3D / canvas:**
- Camera starts at static, close-up view
- Suddenly zooms out to vast, chaotic 3D scene
- Three.js BoxGeometry to InstancedMesh transition

**Scroll pacing:**
- 1:1 scroll-to-zoom ratio
- expo.out easing
- "Shatter" micro-animation on headline

**Micro-copy & CTA:**
- Placed at point of maximum disorientation
- Example: "Enter the Box"
