# Bloodied In-Media-Res Onboarding

**Source:** The Blade Itself • Joe Abercrombie • 2006 • Fantasy
**Motion personality:** Brutal
**Density:** Maximalist
**Psychological lever:** Status threat + loss aversion

---

## How It Works

Drop the user into immediate action without context. Reveal backstory piecemeal.
The curiosity gap is what happened BEFORE the opening scene.

**In the book:** Hero Logen Ninefingers wounded and alone on a riverbank.
No backstory, just immediate crisis. Pulls himself from water, surveys injuries,
sets out into hostile woods. Context revealed piecemeal.

**In web design:** Full-screen dark scene with silhouette at the edge.
Immediate stakes, no explanation.

---

## Web Translation

**Hero / above-the-fold:**
- Full-screen dark-forest or riverbank scene with silhouette at the edge
- Headline: "Still Alive."
- His face/identity and attacker withheld

**Spatial / 3D / canvas:**
- Subtle wobble camera in R3F to simulate breathless POV
- Drifting along a riverbank
- ScrollTrigger reveals bloodied knife and distant danger

**Scroll pacing:**
- Short scroll (200px per event) with fast ease-out (expo.out)
- Micro-animation: heartbeat visualized by slight glow pulse on CTA container

**Micro-copy & CTA:**
- Small text hint ("You must escape…") just above CTA
- Example: "Find Out How"
