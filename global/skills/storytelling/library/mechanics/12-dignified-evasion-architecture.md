# Dignified Evasion Architecture

**Source:** The Remains of the Day • Kazuo Ishiguro • 1989 • Literary Fiction
**Motion personality:** Surgical
**Density:** Minimal
**Psychological lever:** Information asymmetry (what's being avoided) + status/class signaling

---

## How It Works

Use restraint and withholding as the design. Every paragraph is formal, dense
with subordinate clauses. The hook isn't plot — it's watching someone meticulously
not confront what matters. Readers lean in to decode subtext.

**In the book:** Narrator Stevens discusses "great butlers" and staff management.
Every paragraph is restrained, formal. His insistence on "dignity" reveals he's
avoiding emotional reckoning. The reader decodes what's being evaded.

**In web design:** Text-dominant layout with no hero image. Long-form headline
with formal diction. The restraint IS the hook.

---

## Web Translation

**Hero / above-the-fold:**
- Text-dominant layout — no hero image
- Long-form headline (20–30 words), formal diction, subordinate clauses
- Example: "It has occurred to me, on this occasion of reviewing our recent work, that perhaps certain matters deserve reconsideration."
- Withholds what "matters" are

**Spatial / 3D / canvas:**
- Minimal R3F scene — abstract geometric form (hedge maze, overhead view)
- Camera fixed, but maze slowly reconfigures (walls shift via morph targets)
- User cannot interact; only observe transformation

**Scroll pacing:**
- 5x viewport heights to first interactive element
- expo.inOut
- Micro-animation: punctuation marks (commas, em-dashes) fade in 0.3s after surrounding text
- Emphasizes hesitation rhythm

**Micro-copy & CTA:**
- CTA appears deep in page (60%+ scroll)
- Example: "Reconsider with us" — understated, small font, right-aligned
