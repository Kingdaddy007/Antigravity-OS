Last verified: 2026-07-13

Primary source: [ByteDance Seedance 2.0 official launch](https://seed.bytedance.com/blog/seedance-2-0-official-launch), published 2026-02-12.

## Verified

| Capability | Officially stated constraint |
| --- | --- |
| Input modalities | Text, image, audio, and video |
| Image references | Up to 9 |
| Video references | Up to 3 clips |
| Audio references | Up to 3 clips |
| Output | 15-second high-quality multi-shot audio-video output |

| Real-person portrait references | Identity verification or prior legal authorization required |

The official launch also identifies Jimeng AI, Doubao, and the Volcano Ark experience center as launch surfaces. Availability, region, pricing, and controls can change and must be discovered at task time.
